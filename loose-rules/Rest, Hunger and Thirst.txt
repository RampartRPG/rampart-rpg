
  Resting

Resting is an activity done inbetween adventures, It serves many purposes such as relieving stress, restoring HP and many more. It involves the player characters sleeping or laying down.
In RampartRPG there are two methods of resting:

1) Half-Rest: A half-rest takes 2-4 hours of in-game time. Players rest in hostile or unsuitable areas, without proper bedding.
Requirements to take a half-rest:

- There must be no hostile creatures near the players.
- Players must have a surface to sleep or lay down on.
- Players must be in enviroment that allows for survival: no extreme heat or cold. If they are not they can light a campfire or find a shade to go around this restriction.
- Each player needs half a day's worth of rations with them, these are consumed upon a half-rest.
- Each player needs half a day's worth of water with them, these are consumed upon a half-rest.
- Players must rest for 2-4 hours of in-game time if this period is broken they take no benefits from the half-rest.
- Players can have at most two half-rests in a single day.

If all of these conditions are met and the players choose to rest the following happens:

-Players regain HP equal to half their max HP.
-Players lose Stress Points equal to a quarter of their Stress Threshold.
-Players regain any abilites that have an half-rest cooldown.

1) Full-Rest: A full-rest takes 6-8 hours of in-game time. Players rest in adequate areas, with proper bedding.
Requirements to take a full-rest:

- There must be no hostile creatures near the players.
- Players must have some form of bed to sleep or lay down on.
- Players must be either indoors, inside a tent or inside a sleeping bag of some kind.
- Players must be in enviroment that allows for survival: no extreme heat or cold. If they are not they can light a campfire or find a shade to go around this restriction.
- Each player needs one day's worth of rations with them, these are consumed upon a full-rest.
- Each player needs one day's worth of water with them, these are consumed upon a full-rest.
- Players must rest for 6-8 hours of in-game time if this period is broken it is instead counted as a half-rest.
Players can have at most one full-rest in a single day.

If all of these conditions are met and the players choose to rest the following happens:

-Players regain all of their HP.
-Players lose Stress Points equal to half of their Stress Threshold.
-Players regain any abilites that have an half-rest cooldown.
-Players regain any abilites that have an full-rest cooldown.


  Hunger, Thirst and Rations

Hunger and thirst are invisible mechanics in RampartRPG, there are no hard hunger or thirst meters for each player.
Instead rations and water are needed in order to rest. This ties hunger and thirst into the adventuring bits without it being tedious.
Each player can only carry with them 5 + MGT Modifier bags of rations. A bag of rations contains half a day's worth of rations.
Each player can only carry with them 5 + MGT Modifier jugs of water. A jug of water contains half a day's worth of water.
These are consumed upon rest, but they are easy to renew if the players are not in dungeons or other high-stakes enviroments.
In cities players can simply purchase rations with gold and in the wilderness they can hunt and forage with a NATURE skill check.
As for water players do not need to have jugs of water with them while inside cities and towns it is assumed that there is a safe source of drinkable water nearby.
Players can refill all of their water jugs near a clean water source and cities count here too.
If the players rest near a lake or a running source of water it won't consume any jugs of water.
And if they rest near a swamp or a bog a running campfire is enough to purify it once again it won't consume any jugs of water.
Hunger and thirst act as a soft roadblock forcing the players to stop by towns instead mindlessly adventuring for days or heading into a dungeon unprepared.
